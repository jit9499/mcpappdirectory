### Stream 1: Sponsored Listings ($50-200/mo) — Target: $3,000/mo
**What:** MCP server creators pay to have their listing featured, pinned, highlighted
**Tiers:**
| Tier | Price | Placement | Target |
|---|---|---|---|
| Featured Hero | $200/mo | Top of homepage hero carousel | 5 spots = $1,000 |
| Premium Pin | $100/mo | Pinned at top of category | 10 spots = $1,000 |
| Basic Sponsored | $50/mo | "Sponsored" badge + border highlight | 20 spots = $1,000 |
| **Total** | | | **$3,000/mo** |

**Sales approach:** Outbound email to top 100 MCP creators. Free 1-month trial for first 10.

### Stream 2: Verified Badges ($99 one-time) — Target: $1,000/mo
**What:** Official verification checkmark for MCP publishers
**Why they pay:** Trust signal. "Verified" badge shows users this is the official server.
**Target:** 10-15 verifications/mo

### Stream 3: Featured Submissions ($29-99 each) — Target: $1,500/mo
**What:** Paid tier of listing submission
**Tiers:**
- Free: Basic listing (queue, no badge)
- Featured ($29): Expedited review + "New" badge
- Premium ($99): Hero slot for 7 days + newsletter mention
**Target:** 20-30 paid submissions/mo

### Stream 4: Newsletter Sponsorship ($200-500/week) — Target: $2,000/mo
**What:** Single sponsor slot in weekly "MCP Weekly" newsletter
**Pricing:**
- <500 subscribers: Free (build value)
- 500-1,000: $200/week
- 1,000-2,000: $350/week
- 2,000+: $500/week
**Target:** 2,000 subscribers by Month 5 → $2,000/mo

### Stream 5: Job Board ($150 per posting) — Target: $1,500/mo
**What:** "MCP Developer Wanted" — companies hiring MCP developers
**Target:** 10 postings/mo
**Trigger:** Launch when traffic hits 1K+ visitors/day

### Stream 6: Affiliate & Display Ads — Target: $1,000/mo
**What:** Referral fees from MCP hosting platforms, AI tool subscriptions
**Target:** Carbon ads + direct affiliate deals

### Total: $3,000 + $1,000 + $1,500 + $2,000 + $1,500 + $1,000 = $10,000/mo
